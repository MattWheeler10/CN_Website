1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

IT isnt 100% reliable and using technology can have some negative effects and carries risk. Hacking is a huge issue in the IT ecosystem and if you are subject to a hack in the middle of a digital project it can sabotage the whole thing or cause enormous financial burden. Another thing to look out for are viruses as they can slow down or even disable your laptop or pc, making it hard to continue with your project. Beyond that, a common cause of project failure is if the people doing the work don't have the correct knowledge/ skill to complete it as many projects may have difficult aspects that need to be taught to you such as new languages or frameworks, for example Vue.js; a very popular language that is relatively new and combines html, css and javascript in one neat little package. This is why it is essential to have a good team who are qualified in different areas E.G. Project manager and coder. Another problem is overambition and this often occurs when a team expects too much of themselves and they therefore under achieve which is why it is important to be realistic when planning a project.
